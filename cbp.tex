\subsection{Collimated Beam Projector Status}
\label{sec:cbp}

The Collimated Beam Projector (CBP) and Ekspla tunable laser were both installed on the dome during the week
of November 18. See \figRef{cbp_2} They will both be connected to ethernet and a laser interlock system will be installed. First photon from the CBP is projected for December 2.
